About This Extension

Save Image As...

Save any image as PNG, JPG or WebP directly from the right-click menu.

  • Right-click any image and choose the format (PNG, JPG, WebP)
  • Customize JPG and WebP quality (40% to 100%) in the options page
  • Automatically keeps the original filename when possible
  • Multilingual support: English 🇺🇸 and Portuguese 🇧🇷
  • 100% local — no image data is sent to servers
